## Purpose
Customer needs a replacement card due to loss, damage, expiration, or suspected compromise.

## Agent Procedure
1. Confirm the customer's identity using approved synthetic verification steps.
2. Review account status, case history, and related transactions.
3. Apply the category policy: Verify identity, block old card when needed, confirm delivery address and shipping speed.
4. Document the decision, evidence, and any handoff queue.

## Customer-Safe Language
Use plain language. Acknowledge the concern. Explain the next step without promising outcomes that require operations review.

## Escalation
Escalate to Cards Operations when documentation is incomplete, customer sentiment is high-risk, or the request exceeds frontline authority.
